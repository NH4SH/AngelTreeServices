# Local Off-Page SEO Action Plan

## Objective

Strengthen Angel Tree Services' local search authority through accurate, earned references to the canonical website: `https://angeltreeservices.org/`. This is relationship and profile maintenance, not bulk link building.

## Priority Opportunities

1. Confirm the Fredericksburg Regional Chamber of Commerce listing uses the current business name, phone number, and canonical HTTPS URL.
2. Confirm the Fredericksburg Area Builders Association member listing links to the canonical website.
3. Ask Tree Fredericksburg whether an existing acknowledgment or community-support page can link to the company where editorially appropriate.
4. Request a canonical website link from the existing NBC4 coverage only if its editorial correction/contact process permits one.
5. Keep the Best of the Burg finalist profile accurate and linked to the canonical domain where the platform allows it.
6. Maintain an accurate ISA directory/profile link when the currently active credential and directory policy support it.
7. Maintain an accurate ChipDrop company/profile presence where applicable; do not characterize it as a formal partnership.
8. Invite HOA and commercial clients to include Angel Tree Services on approved vendor pages only with permission and accurate relationship wording.
9. Pursue supplier, nonprofit, sponsorship, community, and tree-planting acknowledgments when they reflect real participation.
10. Maintain a small set of accurate local directories with consistent name, phone number, service area, and canonical URL.
11. Earn local media links through useful seasonal safety guidance, storm preparation information, and documented community work.

## Quality Standard

- Prioritize relevant Fredericksburg-area organizations and established relationships.
- Keep the business name, phone number, service area, and URL consistent.
- Prefer editorially earned links and real membership directories.
- Link to `https://angeltreeservices.org/`, not legacy Squarespace or alternate domains.
- Verify each public claim before requesting or accepting a listing update.

## Exclusions

Do not buy backlinks, use private blog networks, automate directory submissions, publish fake guest posts, pursue irrelevant national directories, create reciprocal-link schemes, or offer review incentives tied to links.

No organization should be contacted automatically. Each outreach decision requires owner review and should use the organization's approved profile or editorial process.
