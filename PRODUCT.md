## Design Context

### Users
Primary users are homeowners and commercial or property-management buyers in the Fredericksburg region, including Spotsylvania, Stafford, King George, and Caroline. They typically arrive with a practical need and want to request a quote quickly, while also confirming that the company is credible, experienced, and worth trusting with visible outdoor work on their property.

### Brand Personality
The brand should feel confident, warm, and premium. The voice should be direct, trustworthy, and professional without feeling stiff or corporate. The interface should reduce hesitation, reinforce legitimacy, and make older homeowners feel comfortable moving forward.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use light mode only. The visual direction should stay natural, grounded, and service-oriented, with strong readability and stronger-than-default contrast for older homeowners. Green remains the anchor color family, supported by clean neutrals and restrained accent usage. The design should explicitly avoid generic AI-generated aesthetics, including templated card-grid sameness, trend-chasing visual effects, and flashy styling that weakens trust.

### Design Principles
1. Make requesting a quote feel fast, clear, and low-friction.
2. Prioritize trust signals early through clarity, credentials, and calm visual authority.
3. Keep the interface warm and approachable while preserving a premium, professional finish.
4. Favor strong contrast, readable type, and obvious interactions, especially for older homeowners.
5. Avoid generic AI-looking patterns; every layout and visual treatment should feel intentional and brand-specific.
